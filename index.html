ABOUT

Michael Rafidi

Currently the executive chef of Requin in District Wharf and Arroz in the Mariott Marquis Washington,D.C. Chef creatively updates classic French Cuisine at Requin, and similarly takes a contemporary look at the foods of Southern Spain and Morocco at Arroz. Prior to joining Mike Isabella Concepts, Chef Rafidi served as executive chef at Michael Mina's 3.5 star French restaurant RN74 in San Francisco. His work for th Mina Group included a role as corporate executive sous chef, in which he supported global culinary management of the group's almost 30 properties, as well as opening 8 concepts with Michael Mina.

Chef Rafidi, who grew up in the Washington, D.C. area, & has spent time at other celebrated East Coast restaurants, including Blue Duck Tavern in Washington, D.C., Talula's Garden and The Mildred, both in Philadelphia. He also staged at chef René Redzepi's internationally acclaimed Noma in Copenhagen, Denmark, an experience that fostered his appreciation for foraging and other culinary techniques from around the world.

In January 2017, Chef Rafidi moved back home to Washington, D.C to get back to his roots & cook the cuisine he grew up eating in his grand parents middle eastern kitchen.
